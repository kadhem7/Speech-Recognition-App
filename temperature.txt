Weather Conditions in Tunisian States during World War II:

1. Tunis:
   - Mild Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters.
   - Occasional rainfall during the winter months.
   - Average temperatures range from 10°C (50°F) in winter to 30°C (86°F) in summer.

2. Sousse:
   - Similar to Tunis, Sousse has a Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters.
   - Average temperatures range from 10°C (50°F) in winter to 30°C (86°F) in summer.
   - Sousse experiences slightly less rainfall compared to Tunis.

3. Bizerte:
   - Located in the northern part of Tunisia, Bizerte also has a Mediterranean climate.
   - Summers are hot and dry, while winters are mild and rainy.
   - Average temperatures range from 8°C (46°F) in winter to 28°C (82°F) in summer.
